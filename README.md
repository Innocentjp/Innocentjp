## 🧠 About Me

I'm **Innocent** — a self-taught **Frontend Engineer** and **Product Designer** based in Enugu, Nigeria, building software at the intersection of **civic infrastructure**, **healthcare**, and **product engineering**. I bring a hybrid perspective most engineering profiles don't have: clinical domain literacy from ongoing physiotherapy training, paired with production-grade frontend craft — from custom, framework-free CSS architecture to offline-first PWA design for low-connectivity environments.

My work centers on **React**, **geospatial civic tooling (Leaflet.js)**, full-stack product delivery (offline-first data layers, sync architecture), and **AI-augmented design workflows** — using tools like Figma AI and Stitch AI to compress complex UX requirements into fast, high-fidelity design systems, then engineering the interfaces that ship. I care about interfaces that feel premium and intentional rather than templated, and about software that actually works for the people who need it most, including in contexts with poor connectivity and high stakes.

**Open To:**
- 🎯 Frontend Developer / Software Engineer roles
- 🤝 Freelance & contract product engineering
- 🌍 Civic tech, HealthTech & social-impact collaborations
- 🏆 Hackathons & applied-AI product challenges
